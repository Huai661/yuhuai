Waffle

Waffles are made from a batter or dough cooked between two patterned plates, creating their distinctive ridges and pockets.
These pockets make waffles perfect for holding toppings, from syrups to fruits, or even savory accompaniments.

Types of Waffles we sell:

1. Belgian Waffles

- Known for their deep pockets and light, fluffy texture.
- Often served with whipped cream, fresh fruit, and powdered sugar.

2. American Waffles

- Thinner with smaller pockets.
- Great for breakfast, often paired with butter and syrup or fried chicken.

3. Liege Waffles

- A richer, denser variation with caramelized pearl sugar.
- Commonly enjoyed as a snack or dessert.

4. Savory Waffles

- Made with ingredients like cheese, herbs, or even mashed potatoes.
- Perfect for pairing with eggs, smoked salmon, or stews.
