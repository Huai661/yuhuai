Coffee

The history of coffee dates back over a thousand years.
Legend has it that Ethiopian goat herder Kaldi first discovered the stimulating effects of coffee after noticing his goats becoming energetic after eating red berries from a certain plant.


Types of Coffee we sell:

1. Espresso

- A strong, concentrated shot of coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.
- Forms the base for many other coffee drinks.

2. Americano

- Espresso diluted with hot water, giving it a milder flavor while retaining the espresso boldness.

3. Latte

- A creamy coffee made with one shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam.

4. Cappuccino

- Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, often dusted with cocoa or cinnamon.
