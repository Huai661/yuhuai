Our Services

Free Meal Distribution

We provide free meals to those in need. Our distribution centers are open at the following locations and times:

  • Location 1: 123 Penang, Bayan Lepas. Open every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM.
  • Location 2: 456 Penang, Sungai Ara. Open every Tuesday and Thursday from 1:00 PM to 3:00 PM.

Emergency Assistance

Our organization actively participates in disaster relief efforts. We provide emergency assistance in the form of shelter, food, and medical aid to communities affected by natural or man-made disasters. For more information or to request help, please contact us at huaiiiii@gmail.com.
