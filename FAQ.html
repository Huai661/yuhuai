Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I donate?

Donating is easy! Simply visit our donation page and choose your preferred donation amount. You can donate via credit card, PayPal, or check.

Where does the food come from?

The food we distribute comes from local grocery stores, food banks, and individual donations. We work closely with partners to ensure that all food is fresh and safe for consumption.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers to help with food sorting, distribution, and other activities. Visit our volunteer page to sign up and get more information on how you can help.
