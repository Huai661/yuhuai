Breakfast Toast

Discover our delicious and nutritious toast options, perfect for any time of day!

Avocado Toast

Base: Whole-grain or sourdough bread.
Spread: Mashed avocado with salt, pepper, and lemon.
Toppings: Cherry tomatoes, poached egg, and feta.
Benefits: High in healthy fats and protein.

Nut Butter & Banana Toast

Base: Whole-grain bread.
Spread: Almond, peanut, or cashew butter.
Toppings: Sliced bananas, chia seeds, and honey.
Benefits: A source of healthy fats and energy-boosting carbs.

Greek Yogurt & Berry Toast

Base: Multigrain toast.
Spread: Plain Greek yogurt.
Toppings: Fresh berries and granola.
Benefits: Packed with protein, antioxidants, and probiotics.

Egg & Spinach Toast

Base: Sourdough bread.
Toppings: Sunny-side-up egg, wilted spinach, and pesto.
Benefits: Provides protein and essential nutrients.
